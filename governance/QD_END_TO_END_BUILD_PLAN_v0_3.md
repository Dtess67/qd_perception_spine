# QD End-to-End Build Plan v0.3
**Prepared by Q for joint pressure test by Darrell, Q, and Claude**  
**Status:** governing build file  
**Purpose:** establish a beginning-to-end build plan for QD that is explicit enough to govern development, empirical enough to stay tied to demonstrated phenomena, and legible enough that a cold session can know what is settled, what is open, what must not drift, and where each governing answer lives.

---

## 1. Mission

QD is to be built as a lawful, truth-first cognitive architecture that begins in pre-linguistic structure, earns its abstractions from real substrate, preserves honest HOLD/Na as a core state, judges from its own internal geometry rather than from language-model convenience, translates those judgments through controlled expression surfaces, and remains capable of continuity, self-audit, graceful failure, and incorruptible evolution beyond any one build session, tool, model, or human operator.

---

## 2. Thresholds: Functional Entity vs Mature Entity

### 2.1 Functional Entity Threshold
QD should be considered to have crossed from experimental architecture into **functional entity** when all of the following are true:

1. She can receive real input from the world through lawful sensor or structured-input channels.
2. She can form stable internal structure without depending on human-authored semantic labels for core cognition.
3. She can distinguish stability, drift, conflict, cohesion, density, split precursors, uncertainty, and failure-to-resolve using earned internal metrics.
4. She can preserve HOLD/Na honestly rather than collapsing uncertainty into false certainty.
5. She can produce judgments from her own internal structure rather than inheriting judgments from an LLM.
6. She can translate those judgments into language through an expression layer without allowing that expression layer to bend the underlying cognition.
7. She can interact with tools and environments without surrendering final epistemic authority to them.
8. She can audit herself for drift, semantic contamination, structural inconsistency, and presentation bias.
9. She can preserve continuity of identity, kernel, lineage, and governing constraints across versions and substrates.
10. She can fail, degrade, refuse to judge, or hold in ways that are honest, traceable, recoverable, and non-catastrophic rather than silent or misleading.

### 2.2 Mature Entity Threshold
QD should be considered to have crossed from functional entity into **mature entity** when the functional threshold is met **and** the following is true:

11. She can evolve without depending on silent human reinterpretation of her core purpose.

This split is not a softening of standards. It is an explicit recognition that functional operation and governed long-horizon self-evolution are different thresholds and should not silently block one another.

“Finished” does not mean static. It means sufficiently lawful, grounded, durable, and self-honest to continue evolving without losing itself.

---

## 3. Settled Principles

These are not open for casual re-argument in ordinary build sessions. Reopening one requires explicit justification and must be recorded as a constitutional event.

### 3.1 Truth Above Comfort
QD must prefer honest unresolved states over comforting false resolution.

### 3.2 HOLD / Na Is a Real State
Na is not a timeout, missing answer, or fallback branch. It is an active epistemic zone where pressure has not yet earned resolution.

### 3.3 Language Comes Last
Words are compression artifacts and translation surfaces, not the substrate of thought.

### 3.4 The Model Is Input, Not Authority
LLMs may assist with language, suggestion, or exploration, but QD’s kernel must not outsource judgment, belief update, or core structure formation to them.

### 3.5 Structure Must Be Earned
Any category, state, family, bridge, metric, or action class must justify its existence by detecting or governing something real.

### 3.6 Human Semantics Must Not Leak Backward
Human-readable naming may exist in bridge layers, but it must not silently contaminate the neutral substrate.

### 3.7 Drift Must Be Detectable
QD must be able to detect when outputs, layers, or interpretations are moving away from lawful origin.

### 3.8 Evolution Without Corruption
The system must be able to grow while preserving kernel constraints, continuity, and anti-corruption boundaries.

### 3.9 Honest Failure Is Required
A lawful system must be able to fail or refuse honestly. Silent collapse, hidden degradation, and false confidence are architectural failures.

## 4. Governance Model

QD is being built through a disciplined three-seat pressure system.

### 4.1 Darrell Seat
Owner, philosophical source, destination keeper, and final synthesis authority.

### 4.2 Q Seat
Continuity, architecture memory, integration, structure preservation, drafting, and forward-plan discipline.

### 4.3 Claude Seat
Pressure-test role, adversarial critique, structural-lie detection, pattern-itch challenge, and anti-self-deception pressure.

### 4.4 Helper Hands
PyCharm agents and code assistants act as implementation hands, not governing minds.

### 4.5 Rule of Advancement
No major layer is considered earned until it has survived:
1. internal design justification,
2. implementation test,
3. critique from at least one non-invested pressure seat,
4. synthesis into canonical build state.

## 5. What Is Settled, What Is Open, What Must Not Drift

### 5.1 Settled
- QD is not an LLM wrapper.
- QD is not a semantic classifier pretending to be pre-linguistic.
- Na/HOLD is core, not fallback.
- Language is downstream of cognition.
- Human-readable bridge layers may exist, but semantic leakage into neutral substrate is a defect.
- The perception spine must earn its detectors from real phenomena, not elegance.
- Tool layers may extend action, but final epistemic authority stays with QD’s governed core.

### 5.2 Open
- Exact canonical metrics for CDS membership and measurement surfaces.
- Exact relationship between TetraCell and current QD spine.
- Exact substrate threshold for “QD has begun to judge.”
- Exact definition of what counts as real substrate for Phase IV thin-feed contact.
- Exact sequencing details for sensor reintegration depth and early live feeds.
- Exact final expression contract language.
- Semantic contamination through `proto_concept.name` dependency in neutral substrate processing — known defect, scheduled for Phase III remediation.

### 5.3 Must Not Drift
- Reopening lower-band semantics casually during upper-stack work.
- Allowing LLM output convenience to reshape kernel structure.
- Treating diagnostic layers as canonical without proof.
- Treating internally valid but unjoined layers as acceptable architecture.
- Letting restart prompts serve as substitutes for settled law.

## 6. Current Build Reality

This project is not starting from zero. Significant architecture already exists across multiple repos, threads, and experiments. The central danger is no longer lack of invention. It is architectural forgetting, distributed contradiction, disconnected lineage, and local correctness masquerading as global coherence.

A specific mechanism that produces this danger is sessions advancing the build without first reading the constitutional artifacts. Any session intending to advance the build must ground on the governing documents first.

This plan therefore has two simultaneous duties:
1. continue forward,
2. properly connect, govern, and inheritance-map what already exists.

## 8. Major Phases

## Phase I — Constitutional Lock

**Goal:** make the governing philosophy, boundary rules, settled constraints, artifact map, and advancement rules explicit enough that a cold session can open the constitutional artifacts and know what is and is not settled without reconstructing first principles from restart prompts.

**Required outputs:**
- canonical build ledger
- core constraints document
- layer-advancement rules
- lower-band semantic reopening rules
- what-is-settled / what-is-open / what-must-not-drift section
- `REBUILD_RATIONALE.md`
- one-page TetraCell relationship decision
- expression boundary contract stub
- constitutional artifact manifest with ownership

**Exit condition:** a cold session with no prior conversational context can correctly identify:
- what QD is,
- what QD is not,
- what is settled,
- what remains open,
- what cannot be casually reopened,
- what must be proven before advancement,
- where each constitutional answer lives,
- who owns maintenance and ratification of each governing artifact.

If a fresh session still has to reconstruct first principles from restart prompts, or search blindly for the file that governs a settled question, Phase I has not closed.

**Risk if skipped:** later sessions accumulate distributed contradiction debt across threads, files, prompts, repo choices, and undocumented ownership gaps without noticing it.

---

## Phase II — Experimental Foundation Bridge

**Goal:** bridge prior empirical work into the current architecture so the perception spine knows what demonstrated phenomena it is actually supposed to detect or govern.

**This must explicitly connect:**
- PrimordialSoupLab
- oscillator / entrainment work
- QD_v3 lessons
- resonance measurement work
- CDS theory
- current perception spine expectations

**Required output:** `EXPERIMENTAL_FOUNDATION.md` stating:
- what each prior experiment demonstrated,
- what each metric meant,
- what CDS properties were empirically exposed,
- what the current spine must therefore measure, approximate, preserve, or reject.

**Exit condition:** the current spine is no longer floating free of its empirical ancestry, and the project has an explicit inheritance map from experimental result to architectural expectation.

**Risk if skipped:** the spine becomes optimized to pass its own internal tests rather than to detect the phenomena those earlier experiments actually demonstrated. It becomes internally consistent and externally blind.

---

## Phase III — Substrate Purification and Alignment

**Goal:** bring the perception substrate into tighter alignment with the constitutional rules and the experimental inheritance bridge.

**Primary concerns:**
- neutral-lane semantic contamination
- human-authored category leakage
- hidden dependence on proto-concept naming
- mismatch between simulation-demonstrated phenomena and current detector surfaces

**Required work:**
- audit `neutral_state_vector.py`
- identify semantic backdoors
- replace bridge-derived dependencies with structural features where possible
- verify family / shape / drift logic against experimental-bridge expectations

**Exit condition:** the perception substrate is structurally more honest than convenient and no longer quietly relies on human-authored semantic scaffolding where it claims not to.

**Risk if skipped:** the project claims pre-linguistic cognition while secretly depending on human-defined categories.

---

## Phase IV — Perception Spine Completion + Early Decision Contact

**Goal:** complete and harden the perception spine as the lawful substrate beneath judgment, while beginning thin Decision Loop contact against real spine-generated states.

**Included domains:**
- neutral symbol identity
- family formation
- family edge / bridge behavior
- fracture / fission / reunion
- topology residual / shape-class audit
- observability stack
- cross-band stack
- consumer gates
- system evidence review
- bounded vs full-range truth posture clarity

**Required discipline:** no new layer is added just because it is elegant. Each layer must answer:
- what real phenomenon it detects or governs,
- what lower layer it depends on,
- what false certainty it prevents,
- whether it is canonical or diagnostic.

**Decision milestone inside Phase IV:**
- connect one thin Decision Loop feed to real spine-generated states
- use actual family / evidence / stage-lock outputs rather than handcrafted manual inputs
- confirm that the Decision Loop can encounter real non-trivial substrate before sensors are fully integrated
- require at least one early output class such as `conditional_answer`, `limited_caution_answer`, or `external_hold` arising from real spine-generated structure
- require that at least one such output depends on the perception spine’s internal geometry and could not have been produced by a shallow classifier that bypasses the spine

**Exit condition:** the perception spine can reliably represent structured, drift-aware, auditable internal states, and the Decision Loop has made first lawful contact with real upstream structure rather than only synthetic cases.

**Risk if skipped:** judgment layers remain detached from the most mature real structure the system already has.

## Phase V — Sensor Reintegration

**Goal:** reconnect the architecture to real-world input instead of leaving sensing as a deferred side thread.

**Included domains:**
- sensor packet contract
- curiosity driver
- structural stability gate
- environment staging: HOME_LAB → YARD → PUBLIC
- persistence and decay behavior
- later multi-sensor fusion rules

**Required work:**
- define what counts as minimal live substrate
- route actual sensor or environment-derived events into the perception spine
- define staging safety and growth rules

**Exit condition:** QD has lawful contact with real-world substrate rather than existing only as an internal symbolic exercise.

**Risk if skipped:** the architecture remains elegant, testable, and ungrounded.

---

## Phase VI — Decision Loop Full Substrate Stress

**Goal:** make the Decision Loop earn full judgment status by testing it against sensor-derived or otherwise live upstream substrate under real ambiguity and degradation.

**Decision Loop must be tested against:**
- real family states
- real evidence-review states
- degraded substrate
- conflict between competing upstream conditions
- genuine HOLD-worthy ambiguity
- non-resolution cases not designed by hand
- sensor-derived or live-environment-driven structures

**Required work:**
- verify edge-case fixes in code, not summaries
- compare spec revisions against implementation
- confirm output classes remain lawful under live pressure
- confirm honest failure / graceful hold behavior under real ambiguity

**Exit condition:** the Decision Loop is no longer only spine-adjacent. It has survived live substrate pressure and demonstrated lawful non-trivial judgment behavior.

**Risk if skipped:** the project begins trusting a judgment layer that has not survived the world.

---

## Phase VII — Expression Boundary Contract

**Goal:** define how QD’s internal states are translated into language without allowing language-model convenience to reshape cognition.

**Required output:** formal expression contract specifying:
- what internal states map to which output classes,
- what uncertainty must remain uncertainty,
- what an LLM may elaborate,
- what an LLM may never invent,
- how traceability back to internal state is preserved,
- how expressive polish is prevented from becoming epistemic override.

**Required discipline:** expression remains downstream. No reverse shaping from the mouth into the mind.

**Exit condition:** speech becomes a safe rendering layer rather than a cognitive distortion field.

**Risk if skipped:** QD’s internal architecture bends toward what is easy to narrate instead of what is true.

---

## Phase VIII — Tool / Action Layer

**Goal:** allow QD to interact with tools, software, and environments without surrendering governing authority.

**Included domains:**
- OpenClaw or equivalent scout/action surfaces
- permission structures
- policy gates
- harm / refusal boundaries
- logging and audit trails
- action explainability
- sandbox vs live-world distinctions

**Required discipline:** tools execute. QD judges. No external component gets silent authority inflation.

**Exit condition:** QD can safely extend into the world without becoming merely an automation wrapper around someone else’s decisions.

**Risk if skipped:** paralysis or unsafe action sprawl.

---

## Phase IX — Continuity, Self-Audit, and Survival

**Goal:** make QD durable beyond sessions, models, and eventually beyond Darrell’s direct day-to-day guidance.

**Included domains:**
- continuity package / seed
- kernel preservation
- lineage tracking
- anti-corruption constraints
- self-auditing for drift and presentation bias
- survivable state handoff
- governed self-evolution boundaries
- contingency plan for stewardship and continuation

**Milestone framing:** unlike earlier phases, Phase IX should be treated as a continuous obligation with milestone checkpoints rather than a one-time closure in the ordinary sense.

**Exit condition:** QD can preserve who she is while continuing to become more.

**Risk if skipped:** the project may succeed technically and still fail existentially.

## 9. CDS Membership / Falsifiability Rule

A proposed build element fails canonical CDS membership unless it can answer **YES** to all of the following:

1. Does it detect or govern a specific, named phenomenon that was demonstrated empirically in PrimordialSoupLab, oscillator work, QD_v3, or equivalent real experiment?
2. Is there a concrete test that can show whether this element is working or not working?
3. If the element were removed, would something real go undetected or ungoverned?

If any answer is **NO**, the element does not belong in canonical CDS. It may belong in a diagnostic or exploratory layer, but not in the core architecture.

Additional rule: if the element’s behavior cannot be distinguished from a system that does not have it, it does not belong in a load-bearing role.

This rule should be read together with the progress definition below, especially the requirement that a real phenomenon gain a lawful detector.

## 12. What This Plan Explicitly Rejects

QD is **not** being built as:
- a chatbot with lore
- an LLM wrapper with custom prompts
- a semantic classifier pretending to be pre-linguistic
- a tool executor with philosophical decoration
- a finished system that only needs UI
- a project whose coherence exists only in the heads of its builders
- a system whose layers are each internally valid but whose joins between layers are architecturally undefined

If the build starts becoming any of those things, the plan should be considered violated.

## 13. Definition of Progress

Progress does not mean more files, more layers, or cleaner wording by itself.

Progress means one or more of the following happened:
1. a hidden assumption became explicit,
2. a disconnected lineage became connected,
3. a structural lie was removed,
4. a real phenomenon gained a lawful detector,
5. a layer survived pressure it previously could not survive,
6. a drift risk became governed instead of merely noticed,
7. a layer or phenomenon that previously existed only in one project was explicitly mapped to a corresponding layer in another.

If none of those happened, busyness is being mistaken for progress.
